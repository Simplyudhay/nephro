A Medical First in India

Dr. Raja Mahesh managed a groundbreaking case of a 26-year-old woman from Tiruvannamalai with renal failure and SLE (Systemic Lupus Erythematosus) who was on haemodialysis for 2 years. Against all odds, she delivered a healthy baby weighing 2.1 kg after being on dialysis 6 days a week throughout her pregnancy.

This remarkable achievement demonstrated that with expert medical care, advanced nephrology techniques, and unwavering dedication, even the most challenging cases can have successful outcomes.

"With less than 10% kidney function, this case demonstrated that compassionate care and medical excellence can achieve the impossible."
